## Main announcement (T-3 days, post today)

**[Heads-up] OpenStack 2026.1 upgrade**

The platform will upgrade OpenStack from 2025.2 to 2026.1 starting **Sunday 5/24 at 21:00 (UTC+8)**. Expected to complete within 150 minutes, end-of-window 24:00 the same day.

**Unavailable during the window:**
- Horizon <https://openstack.cloudnative.tw>
- Skyline <https://console.cloudnative.tw>
- All OpenStack APIs (including `openstack` CLI, Terraform, CI/CD automation)

**Not affected during the window:**
- Running instances keep running (no reboot, no migration)
- Tenant network, Floating IP, Load Balancer, outbound connectivity stay up
- Existing Cinder volume attachments and Swift object I/O continue

**One-time effect after the upgrade:**
- All Horizon / Skyline sessions are invalidated (session backend switches to Valkey). Please log in again. Passwords are unchanged.

**Recommended preparation:**
- Pause deploys / CI / Terraform apply between 5/24 21:00–24:00.
- Save drafts of any in-progress Horizon forms.
- Keep your usual backups for important data (this remains a community lab environment).

Live updates will be posted to this channel. Issues: `infra@cloudnative.tw`.
